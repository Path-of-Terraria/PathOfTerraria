~Not intended to be played with other Content mods. QOL mods are fine~

Path of Terraria is a fresh take on the world of Terraria bringing in ARPG elements.
Find and help rebuild towns, complete quests, and explore the world of Terraria in a new exciting way.

Path of Terraria is fully multiplayer-compatible and can be played with friends or solo.

New Content Includes
- Global Leaderboard System (With Online Mod)
- Global Trade System (With Online Mod)
- Towns
- Questing
- Passive Tree
- Skills
- Leveling
- Completely new way to progress (Boss Domains)
- Item Modifiers (Affixes)
- Item Crafting System
- Mapping Endgame System
- Several New Bosses
- Several new NPCs
- Several new Enemies
